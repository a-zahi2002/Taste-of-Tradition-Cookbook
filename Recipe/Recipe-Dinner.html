Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and onions, sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes, cook until soft.
  3. Add chicken, curry powder, and salt, cook for 20 minutes.
  4. Simmer until the chicken is cooked and serve with rice or flatbread.

Details

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4

Beef Fry

Ingredients

  • 500g beef, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add onions, and sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, beef, and spices, cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Simmer until beef is tender and well-cooked.
  4. Serve hot with flatbread or rice.

Details

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Dal Tadka

Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the dal with turmeric until soft, set aside.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and onions, sauté until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes, red chilies, and salt, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the boiled dal and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or roti.

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Prawn Masala

Ingredients

  • 500g prawns, cleaned
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add onions and sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes, cook until soft.
  3. Add prawns, curry powder, turmeric, and salt, cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes and serve with rice.

Details

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4

Mixed Vegetable Curry

  • 1 tsp turmeric powder

Vegetable Pulao

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, beans)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil rice and set aside.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, bay leaf, and cloves, sauté for a minute.
  3. Add onions and mixed vegetables, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add cooked rice, salt, and mix well.
  5. Simmer for 5 minutes and serve hot.

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Fish Fry

Ingredients

  • 4 fish fillets
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate fish with ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, coriander, and salt.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry fish until golden brown.
  3. Serve hot with lemon wedges.

Details

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4

Lamb Stew

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb, cubed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add onions, and sauté until golden.
  2. Add lamb, tomatoes, and spices, cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Add carrots and water, simmer for 1 hour until lamb is tender.
  4. Serve hot with rice or bread.

Details

Cooking Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Servings: 4
