Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice/li>
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, potatoes)
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook basmati rice until fluffy and set aside
  2. Heat ghee in a pot, add cumin and onions, sauté until golden
  3. Add vegetables, biryani masala, and salt, cook for 10 minutes
  4. Layer the rice over the vegetable mixture, cover, and steam for 10 minutes
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with raita

Details

Cooking Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 4

Mutton Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and onions, sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes, cook until soft.
  3. Add mutton, curry powder, turmeric, and salt, cook until browned.
  4. Add water, cover, and simmer for 40 minutes until the mutton is tender.
  5. Serve with rice or roti.

Details

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter, add onions, and sauté until soft.
  2. Add tomato puree, garam masala, turmeric, and salt, cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in cream and garnish with coriander.
  5. Serve with naan or rice.

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Chickpea Curry (Chana Masala)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and onions, sauté until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes, turmeric, garam masala, and salt, cook until tomatoes soften.
  3. Add boiled chickpeas and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or flatbread.

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Spinach Dal (Palak Dal)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the toor dal with turmeric until soft, set aside.
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, onions, and green chilies, sauté until soft.
  3. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  4. Stir in the boiled dal and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve with rice or roti.

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Vegetable Khichdi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Wash rice and dal together, then cook with water until soft.
  2. Heat ghee, add cumin seeds and vegetables, sauté for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the cooked rice and dal mixture, turmeric, and salt.
  4. Cook for another 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve with pickle or yogurt.

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Fish Curry

  • 500g fish, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 tbsp coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds and onions, sauté until golden.

Eggplant Fry (Baingan Bharta)

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Roast the eggplant over an open flame until the skin is charred, peel, and mash it.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and onions, sauté until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes, garam masala, turmeric, and salt, cook until soft.
  4. Stir in the mashed eggplant and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve with flatbread or rice.

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook basmati rice until fluffy and set aside.
  2. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, biryani masala, and salt for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat ghee in a pot, add cumin and onions, sauté until golden.
  4. Add marinated chicken, cook for 15 minutes.
  5. Layer the rice over the chicken, cover, and steam for 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with raita.

Details

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Servings: 4
