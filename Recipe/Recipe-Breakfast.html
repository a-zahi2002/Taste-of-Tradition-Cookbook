Paratha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Water as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, oil, and water to form a soft dough
  2. Divide the dough into balls, roll them out, and fold with ghee
  3. Roll out again and fry on a hot griddle with ghee until golden and crispy.
  4. Serve with yogurt or pickles

Details

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4

Upma

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Curry leaves
  • 2 Cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Roast semolina in a pan until golden, set aside
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, onion, chili, and curry leaves, sauté
  3. Add water and salt, bring to a boil
  4. Stir in the roasted semolina, cook until thickened
  5. Serve hot with chutney

Details

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4

Idli

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 2 cups rice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak urad dal and rice separately for 4-6 hours, grind to a smooth batter
  2. Ferment overnight, add salt, and pour into idli molds
  3. Steam for 10-12 minutes until fluffy
  4. Serve with coconut chutney and sambar

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes (excluding fermentation)

Servings: 4

Puttu

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Mix rice flour, grated coconut, and salt
  2. Add water gradually until the mixture resembles wet sand
  3. Layer the mixture in a puttu steamer and steam for 10-12 minutes
  4. Serve with bananas and curry

Details

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4

Aloo Paratha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Ghee for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough with wheat flour and water
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with cumin, chili, and salt
  3. Roll out the dough, stuff with potato mixture, and roll again
  4. Fry on a hot griddle with ghee until crispy
  5. Serve with yogurt and pickles

Details

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4

Chana Dal Pancakes (Cheela)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Soak chana dal for 4 hours, grind into a thick batter
  2. Mix in onions, chili, cumin, and salt
  3. Heat oil on a griddle and pour a ladle of batter, spread into a thin pancake
  4. Cook on both sides until golden
  5. Serve with chutney

Details

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4

Chole Bhature

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Spices: turmeric, chili powder, garam masala
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, and yogurt to form a dough
  2. For chole, sauté cumin, onion, and spices, add chickpeas and cook
  3. Roll out the dough and deepfry into puffed bhature
  4. Serve with chole (spicy chickpea curry)

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Dhokla

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tbsp semolina
  • 1 tbsp eno fruit salt
  • 1 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 1 green chilli, chopped
  • salt to taste
  • water as needed

Instructions

  1. Mix gram flour, semolina, salt, and water into a thick batter
  2. Add eno fruit salt just before steaming
  3. Pour into a greased dish and steam for 15 minutes
  4. Heat oil, mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chili, and pour over the dhokla
  5. Serve with chutney

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Dhokla

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Soak urad dal for 4 hours, grind into a thick batter
  2. Mix in onions, chili, cumin, and salt
  3. Shape the batter into doughnuts and deep fry until golden
  4. Serve with chutney or sambar

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4
