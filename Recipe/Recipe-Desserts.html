Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic, and saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper, then simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Toss the pasta in the sauce and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve.

Details

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Serve immediately.

Details

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 2
